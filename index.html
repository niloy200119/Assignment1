Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the relentless pursuit of better solutions, where imagination meets courage to reshape the ordinary into the extraordinary.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the harmonious dance between progress and preservation, ensuring that the beauty and resources of today endure for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the backbone of civilization, a testament to human ingenuity that connects dreams to reality and lays the foundation for a brighter future.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the spark that ignites the human spirit, enabling individuals to break barriers, embrace their potential, and shape a world filled with opportunity and equity.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh made a historic leap in space technology with the successful launch of its first satellite, Bangabandhu-1, on May 11, 2018. Named after the nation's founding father, Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, the satellite was launched from the Kennedy Space Center in Florida using a SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket. It is a geostationary communications satellite designed to improve telecommunications, broadcasting, and internet services across the country, including remote and rural areas. The project signifies Bangladesh's entry into the elite space club and showcases its ambition in science and technology.
